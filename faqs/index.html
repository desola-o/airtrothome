Everything you need to know about how we plan, structure, and coordinate your perfect trip.

Airtrot is a travel concierge service that helps you plan, structure, and coordinate your trip from start to finish. We provide guidance, curated options, and support throughout the process so you don’t have to manage everything on your own.
You start by sharing your travel details through our form. We review your request and begin structuring your trip. From there, we guide you through the next steps and coordinate the details needed to bring your trip together.
The concierge fee covers the time, research, and coordination required to structure your trip. This includes tailored recommendations, itinerary planning, and guided support throughout the process. The concierge will be applied toward your final booking.
